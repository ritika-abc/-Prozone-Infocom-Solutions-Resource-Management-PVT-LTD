A Boom Barrier is an effective traffic control solution ideal for managing vehicle access in parking lots, toll booths, and gated areas. It allows for controlled access while ensuring safety and smooth traffic flow.

  • Features:
    • Automatic Operation
    • Durability and Reliability
    • Customizable Design
    • Safety Features
  • Applications:
    • Parking Lots
    • Toll Booths
    • Access Control Areas

Bollard Barrier

A Bollard Barrier is designed to protect buildings, storefronts, and public areas by restricting vehicle access while maintaining pedestrian flow. These barriers provide strong protection without compromising on aesthetics.

  • Features:
    • Protection and Safety
    • Customizable Materials
    • Aesthetic Designs
    • Retractable Options
  • Applications:
    • Urban Spaces
    • Parking Areas
    • Industrial Zones

Spike Barrier

Spike Barriers are designed to prevent unauthorized vehicle access by puncturing tires. These barriers are an excellent security feature for high-risk areas that require immediate stoppage of vehicles.

  • Features:
    • High Security
    • Retractable or Fixed Spikes
    • Durability
    • Easy Integration
  • Applications:
    • Military Installations
    • Government and Embassy Buildings
    • Private Properties and Gated Communities
